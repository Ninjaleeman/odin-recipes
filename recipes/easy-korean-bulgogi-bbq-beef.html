Easy Bulgogi Korean BBQ Beef Recipe

Bulgogi Korean BBQ Beef is a delicious and delectable meal. It's also super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 Green Onions, Chopped with greens and whites separated.
  • 1/4 Yellow Onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons White Sugar
  • 3 cloves minced Garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon minced Ginger
  • pinch of ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 pound beef sirloin steak, cut very thin
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Whisk soy sauce, white and light green parts of green onions, yellow onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, ginger, and black pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl. Add steak slices and toss to evenly coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour, or up to 1 day.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a wok or large skillet over medium heat. Working in batches, cook and stir steak and marinade together in the hot skillet, adding honey to caramelize the steak, until steak is cooked through, about 5 minutes. Garnish bulgogi with the green parts of green onions.
